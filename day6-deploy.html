Video Attributes

Control your media with these attributes:

  • controls: Displays playback controls
  • autoplay: Starts playback automatically (may be blocked by browsers)
  • loop: Repeats the media continuously
  • muted: Mutes the audio by default
  • poster: Specifies an image to show before playback
  • width and height: Set the dimensions of the player

Accessibility with Tracks

Make your media accessible with captions and subtitles:

<video controls>
  <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  <track src="captions_en.vtt" kind="captions" srclang="en" label="English">
  <track src="subtitles_es.vtt" kind="subtitles" srclang="es" label="Spanish">
</video>
